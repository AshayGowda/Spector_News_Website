The latest data released by the National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) shows that the total number of riot victims in India increased by 22 per cent in 2017, even though the number of rioting incidents saw a decrease of 5 per cent in comparison to the previous year. NCRB's 'Crime in India 2017' report that was released on Monday, shows that in 2017, India witnessed 58,880 rioting cases while the number of riot victims stood at 90,394. In comparison to this, a year ago the number of rioting cases was 61,974 and the number of victims was 73,744 (i.e. 169 riots and 202 victims every day).
